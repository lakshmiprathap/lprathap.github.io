Syllabus covered

NFS (Need For Speed Maths) and Logical Puzzles Workshop:

A customised workshop designed for students to help them improve their calculation speed and invoke their Logical thinking ability. Various Speed Techniques include Addition & Subtraction, Squares, Square roots and cube roots, Multiplication, Divisibility rules, Percentages calculations, Ratio basics, etc…

Quantitative Ability and Data Interpretation:

Ratios & Proportions, Partnerships, Equations & Ages, Numbers, Averages, Percentages, Profit & Loss, Mixtures & Allegations, Simple & Compound interest, Time & Work, Pipes & Cisterns, Time, Speed & Distance, Permutations & Combinations, Probability, Bar graphs, Line graphs, Pie charts, Tabular based DI.

Logical Reasoning and Data Sufficiency:

Data Arrangements, Syllogisms, Blood relations, Cubes, Clocks, Coding & Decoding, Calendars, Directions, Coded Inequalities, Venn Diagrams, Data Sufficiency (Quant & Logical Based)

Special Orientation on Psychometric tests and Interviews:

Insights on Psychometric tests, necessary preparation plan for cracking Interviews along with Trainer’s Interview experience.

High-Level Aptitude covering additional topics:

Functions, Advanced Number systems, Advanced Permutations and Combinations, Progressions, Sequence and Series, Geometry and Mensuration.
